What acadia national park offers!

Sandy Beach

Sandy is home to the cold waters of maine. Sandy beach contains multiple trails that lead to marvelous views of the beach.

Cadillac Mountain

Cadillac Mountain brings you to heights you never imagined. By the end of this 2.2 mile hike, you'll get an extraordinary view of the National park. Best times to arrive is during sunrise and sunset.

Bee Hive Trail

The Bee Hive trails is a difficult climb, but contains one of the best views. By the end of this 1.4 miles hike, you can see Sandy Beach

Thunder Hole

The Thunder Hole is one of acadias fascinating places to be seen. Containing a wide varations of rock formation, tourist would be wowed to see it. During high tide, the Thunder Hole is capable of splashing toursit with its sea water

Bar Harbor

Bar Harbor brings you to heights you never imagined. By the end of this 2.2 mile hike, you'll get an extraordinary view of the National park. Best times to arrive is during sunrise and sunset.

Maine is well known for its lobsters. Try some lobster at Stewman's Lobster Pound, one of the best there is in Bar Harbor
